Cake & Other Booking Information

Booking your perfect cake with us is easy! Simply contact us via phone at 020 7946 0765 or email contact@watsontreats.co.uk. to discuss your requirements. Whether you're after a classic design or something custom, we offer a variety of flavors, sizes, and decoration options to suit your occasion. Please allow at least two weeks' notice for standard orders, though we can accommodate last-minute requests when possible (rush fees may apply). A 50% deposit is required upon booking, with the remaining balance due at pickup or delivery. We offer both pickup from our shop and delivery options within a set area—please inquire for details. For cancellations, we kindly ask for 72 hours' notice for a full refund. If you have any special requests or questions, don't hesitate to reach out! We're happy to help bring your cake vision to life.
